My First Day At NSS

My first day at NSS. May 21st, 2018

The day has finally come. Today is the first day of Cohort 26. Today we had introductions and a few hundred motivational slides. I feel fear and excitment for what will come. The one thing that I realize today is you can never do enough pre-work. Things will be moving fast for sure. That's ok, I have a strategy. That is to remain calm and stay the course. I know this is going to be extremely tough but, the reward will be great. Coding to me is a combination of logic and creativity. The main driving force for me to learn coding is to create solutions to problems and or create an application that is useful and scalable. I can't wait to see what tomorrow brings.

Michael Roberts May 21st 2018
